High School Admissions

Welcome to Our Admissions Page

We are excited to welcome new students to our high school. Please read the information below to learn more about our admissions process and requirements.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Transcripts from previous schools
  • Recommendation letters
  • Entrance exam results

Application Process

  1. Submit your application form online or in person.
  2. Attend an interview with our admissions team.
  3. Take the entrance exam (if applicable).
  4. Receive your admission decision.
  5. Complete the enrollment paperwork and pay the registration fee.

Contact Us
